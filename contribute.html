How to Help

Many resources can be found to help contribute to the BLM cause. Listed below are some links with more information and resources from the Black Lives Matter site.

Donate

People donate their time, money, and talents to contribute to the BLM movement. You can take part whether it be actively protesting in the streets or raising awareness on social media.

Get Educated

Find reliable resources and don't be afraid to get your hands dirty and do some research of your own

Fight Disinformation

Report suspicious sites, stories, ads, social accounts, and posts about BLM.

Social Media

Follow BLM on social media and keep yourself updated on important milestones from reliable resources.
