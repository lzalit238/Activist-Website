The Movement

#BlackLivesMatter is an all-inclusive hashtag used to spread awareness about Black communities which includes, trans, disabled, and underprivileged people. It is used to fight oppression and laws that are systemically targeted towards the Black community's demise.

Black Lives Still Matter

The #BlackLivesMatter was started 6 years ago and it is highly important to keep fighting for the liberation of the Black Community.

Protesting

There are many forms of protesting used in the BLM movement which range from walking the streets of your city to simply signing petitions.

Juneteenth

Juneteenth celebrates African-American liberation and it is celebrated all across the country. It has recently been made a national holiday by President Joe Biden.

Expansive

Black Lives Matter is a collective group of liberators who is focused on being an all-inclusive and spacious movement.
